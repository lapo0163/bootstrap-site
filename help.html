8 Steps to making a better environment

  1. Shut off lights when you leave a room
  2. Unplug devices when not in use
  3. Avoid drinking out of plastic and use a reusable water bottle
  4. Instead of plastic bags, bring your own reusable shopping bag
  5. Reduce, Reuse Recycle
  6. Don't litter
  7. Bike, take public transit or carpool with a co-worker or a friend
  8. Visit local food markets and buy organic
